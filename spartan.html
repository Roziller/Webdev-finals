Overview

The Spartan Workout Plan is forged in discipline, power, and unwavering resilience — a program designed to push you beyond your limits and build the mindset and physique of a warrior. Inspired by the legendary Spartan warriors, this training regimen will hone your strength, endurance, and combat readiness, preparing you to face any battle — on or off the field.

Instructions
  • Use the database to find a workout that matches the type assigned for the day.
  • Set the difficulty level (1 to 5) to match your current fitness level.
  • Pick a workout and complete it on Level I, II, or III, depending on how much time and energy you have.
  • On rest days, we recommend you complete a stretching routine or a yoga sequence to help keep your exercise streak going.
  • Add an ab workout to support mobility and core strength.
