What is VIGILANT?

Vigilant is a safety-focused web application designed to keep communities informed and secure. By allowing users to report areas of criminal activity, Vigilant ensures others are promptly alerted when entering potentially dangerous zones. Utilizing real-time notifications similar to typhoon alerts, the platform warns users if they are within a high-crime radius. Additionally, Vigilant offers a Life360-style feature, enabling users to share their real-time locations with trusted contacts for added security.
