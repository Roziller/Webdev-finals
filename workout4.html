Forge strength with every rep. Muscle Alchemy is a dumbbell-based strength workout built around combination moves that challenge multiple muscle groups at once. Think functional patterns that mirror real-world movement and maximize efficiency.

This is not just a workout - it’s a transformation ritual. You’ll build strength, coordination, and balance while keeping your heart rate up. Whether you're short on time or looking to supercharge your training, this workout turns effort into results.

Extra Credit: Use 10 lb+ (5 kg+) dumbbells minimum.
