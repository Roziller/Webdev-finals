Start the day swinging. Morning Bell is a kettlebell-powered wake-up call that gets your blood flowing and muscles firing. With a focus on swings, this routine builds strength, boosts circulation, and charges up your energy for the day ahead.

Simple, rhythmic, and explosive, kettlebell swings work your glutes, core, and shoulders while giving your entire system a powerful jolt - no caffeine required. Pair it with sunrise and you're unstoppable.

Extra Credit: 1 minute rest between sets.
