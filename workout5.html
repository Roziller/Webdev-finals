Looks simple - burns like fire. Don’t let the name fool you. Nothing Fancy is a sleek, deceptively tough metcon routine packed with advanced push-up and squat variations that target strength, endurance, and control.

It’s bodyweight-only but far from basic, demanding focus, stability, and grit. Clean design, dirty burn. If you're craving that deep, satisfying workout feel without any equipment or flair - this is it.
