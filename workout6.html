Strong, steady, and full of purpose, this workout is all about building power from the ground up. It focuses on control, muscle engagement, and holding your own through every rep. There’s no rushing - just deliberate movement designed to challenge and empower you.

Ideal for days when you want to feel grounded and capable, Shieldbreaker invites you to push your limits while staying present in your body. It’s strength training at its purest - unfiltered and deeply satisfying.
